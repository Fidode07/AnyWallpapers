AnyWallpaper's

Everything your Desktop needs



Use videos and GIFs as wallpaper and bring your desktop to life with them.



Supported Platforms:


Built for performance

As soon as you maximize an application or start a game, the wallpaper on the respective screen is automatically paused. This way you get your performance as soon as you need it.


Multiple screen support

AnyWallpaper's can easily cope with multiple screens. The resolution and aspect ratio do not matter.


Choose your style

You have found a cool video, but for example the color does not suit you? All good, the engine has things like hue-rotate, sepia, contrast and more, so you can edit everything the way you want.


Open Source

AnyWallpaper's is open source and licensed under the GNU General Public License v3.0. You can find the source code on GitHub.


Easy to modify

The engine is written in html. What does that mean? You can easily change the design of your engine the way you want it.


YouTube Support

Yes, you heard right. The engine also supports YouTube videos. With this feature, you'll never have to download a video again. Stream comfortably via the URL.
